This page is for learning and building the Rock, Paper, Scissors Game.

The entire game is played through your browser's console using the game() function. There will be a future version to play this game without the need to go into the console.

To play my Rock, Paper, Scissors game, please follow the instructions below:

  • Open your browser's console.
  • Type `game()` (without the `'s) to start the game function.
  • The game will run 5 times and a winner will be announed in the console.
  • Play as many times as you want.
